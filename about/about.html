My goals

Motivated by a pursuit of excellence, my career aspirations are anchored in the desire to contribute to esteemed organisations such as Avalanche Studios, renowned for their groundbreaking contributions to the gaming industry. With a meticulous focus on aesthetic refinement and user-centric design principles, my goal is to develop interfaces that seamlessly integrate with captivating gameplay, enriching a player's journey and fostering unforgettable experiences.

I love travelling and my biggest fear is to have an office job where I don't enjoy what I do, thus I hope to work remotely and get opportunities to work all around the world. Whether collaborating with international teams or immersing myself in different environments, my aim is to cultivate a global network of colleagues and collaborators, drawing inspiration from the rich tapestry of perspectives and experiences that our world has to offer.

In pursuit of academic and professional excellence, I would love to pursue a Ph.D., delving into the depths of interactive design theory and pushing the boundaries of knowledge within the field. I envision myself contributing to the academic discourse while simultaneously applying my insights to real-world projects. Through this approach, I aim to bridge the gap between theory and practice, ultimately redefining the future of interactive entertainment with innovation and expertise.
