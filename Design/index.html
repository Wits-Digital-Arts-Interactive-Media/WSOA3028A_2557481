My User Flow Diagram

Looking at my user flow diagram, you can see that users have access to every main page in my website. Having a navigation bar at the top of a website is a fundamental UI and UX choice that significantly enhances user experience and flow. Primarily, it provides users with immediate access to essential sections or pages of the website, allowing for effortless navigation. With the navigation bar consistently positioned at the top, users can quickly orient themselves and understand the structure of the site, reducing confusion and frustration. This enhances usability, especially for first-time visitors who may not be familiar with the website's layout.

Moreover, a fixed navigation bar ensures that users can easily move between pages or sections without having to scroll back to the top, saving time and effort. This seamless navigation experience contributes to a positive overall user experience, increasing engagement and satisfaction with the website. Additionally, a well-designed navigation bar with clear labels and intuitive placement fosters a sense of trust and credibility, as users perceive the website as organized and user-friendly. Therefore, incorporating a navigation bar at the top of the website is not only a good practice but also a critical element in optimizing UI and UX design.

My Style Guide

The style guide for my website is meticulously designed to evoke a sense of classiness and formality while ensuring accessibility and aesthetic appeal. The primary font used throughout the site is Bodoni MT, chosen for its elegant and timeless appearance. This font, consistently applied at a 25px size, maintains readability across all pages, reinforcing a coherent and professional look. The color scheme features a dark grey background contrasted with light beige font color, creating a subtle yet effective contrast that enhances readability without straining the eyes. Additionally, colors derived from the artwork featured on the website are selectively incorporated, seamlessly blending the site's elements and artwork for a cohesive visual experience.

The use of large artworks by various impressionist artists aims to mimic the atmosphere of a museum, with the website serving as a guide. This approach not only showcases the beauty of the art but also provides a calming and visually rich experience for users. The neutral color palette, seen in the photographs and artwork, contributes to a serene and inviting ambiance, promoting a sense of tranquility and focus. By integrating these elements, the website offers a visually pleasing environment that encourages users to explore and engage with the content without feeling overwhelmed or distracted.

The consistency in design elements, such as font choice, color palette, and layout, significantly enhances the website's effectiveness and accessibility. The readability of Bodoni MT at 25px ensures that all text is easily legible, while the dark grey background and light beige font provide optimal contrast, adhering to accessibility standards. This thoughtful design consideration ensures that users with visual impairments or reading difficulties can navigate and consume the content comfortably. The incorporation of colors from the featured artwork not only ties the visual elements together but also maintains a harmonious and aesthetically pleasing experience.

Furthermore, the strategic placement of the navigation bar at the top of each page ensures that users can easily access different sections of the site, enhancing the overall user experience. The inclusion of a smooth scroll-up button adds to the site's navigational efficiency, allowing users to return to the top of the page effortlessly. The large images, central to the museum-like experience, are not only visually engaging but also help in creating a clear and structured layout. This design choice, combined with the neutral and calming color palette, ensures that the website is both functional and inviting, providing users with a seamless and enjoyable browsing experience.

My Colour Palette

Similarly, employing colours such as grey and beige on my website contributes to its visual appeal and user experience. Beige, on the other hand, evokes feelings of warmth and tranquility, creating a welcoming atmosphere that encourages user engagement and exploration. Together, these colours creates a harmonious and balanced colour palette that conveys professionalism and professionalism to my website.
